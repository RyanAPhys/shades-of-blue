Shades of Blue

Jazz sextet based in Enfield, North London

Who we are

Shades Of Blue is a friendly and professional jazz sextet playing a mix of vocal and instrumental jazz and swing standards infused with blues and Latin music.

Based in Enfield, North London, their gigs are varied and include parties, pubs, corporate events, open air venues and even school outreach work across London. If you are looking for a jazz band to make your event swing, please contact us for a quote using the information below.
